Features

Built for every stakeholder

Students get clarity. Colleges get data. Recruiters get signal.

A live ML score. Not a static resume rating.

Updates when you push to GitHub, clear a mock interview, earn a cert, or attend a workshop. Think CIBIL score — but for student employability. Powered by a HuggingFace transformer fine-tuned on 50,000+ JD-profile pairs with a scikit-learn scoring pipeline.

Knows exactly what you're missing. And tells you.

Pulls live JD data from Naukri, LinkedIn, and Internshala. Compares against your profile using a scikit-learn scoring pipeline. Generates a weekly action plan specific to the role you want — not a generic "learn DSA" suggestion.

How it works

From login to verified credential

1
Student logs in

Score loads instantly. Dashboard shows current gaps, pending actions, and recent activity.

2
Skill gaps identified

ML scrapes live JDs, compares against your profile, and generates a weekly action plan.

3
Mock interview taken

Whisper transcribes. MediaPipe tracks posture. Score updates in real time on the dashboard.

4
Credential minted

Achievement minted as an NFT on Polygon. Recruiter verifies with one click. No calls needed.
